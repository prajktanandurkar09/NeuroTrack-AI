## 📖 Overview

NeuroTrack AI is an **AI-powered Academic Intelligence Platform** designed to transform traditional educational systems into intelligent, data-driven learning environments.

Unlike conventional ERP systems that only manage attendance and records, NeuroTrack AI integrates **Artificial Intelligence**, **Computer Vision**, and **Learning Analytics** to understand classroom engagement, monitor student emotions, automate attendance, provide AI assistance, and improve academic decision-making.

> **"Understanding Minds. Improving Learning."**

